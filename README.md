# 无欲 (Abstain)

**无欲** is a self-discipline and personal growth companion app.

Build better habits, track your daily progress, and connect with a supportive community — all with complete privacy.

## Features

- **Daily Check-in** — Track your streaks and celebrate milestones
- **Community** — Share your journey, give and receive encouragement (anonymous mode available)
- **Knowledge Center** — 10,000+ articles on habit formation, self-discipline, mindfulness, and more
- **Journal** — Track your energy, mood, focus, sleep, and exercise
- **Accountability Partners** — Designate trusted contacts for extra support

## Privacy

All data is stored locally on your device. No account required. No data uploaded to servers.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Go to Profile → Account Settings → Delete Account. All local data will be cleared.

**Q: Is my data stored on a server?**
A: No. All data is stored locally on your device. We do not have servers.

**Q: Do I need to create an account?**
A: No account or registration is required. You can use the app immediately.
