# 无欲 Abstain — Support

**无欲 (Abstain)** is a pure offline self-discipline tool for building healthy habits.

## Privacy

All data is stored locally on your device. No registration, no internet connection required. Your data never leaves your device.

## Account Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time:
1. Open the app
2. Go to **Profile** tab
3. Tap **Delete Account**
4. Confirm twice

This will permanently remove all your check-in records, journal entries, and settings.

## FAQ

**Q: Is this app free?**
A: Yes, completely free with no ads.

**Q: Does the app require an internet connection?**
A: No, the app works fully offline. No network permissions are needed.

**Q: Where is my data stored?**
A: All data is stored locally on your device using the app's local storage.

**Q: How do I reset my data?**
A: Go to Profile → Reset All Data.
